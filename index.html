23/05/2006: Sunflower Day

Dear Diary, today I played with a new friend. They call it a sunflower. It's yellow in color and all the bees are excited. They put the sunflower on a raft and sailed the ocean for eons to come! I watched from my throne aka the living room sofa as the bees buzzed in circles, clearly impressed by my calm composure. Later, I tried to take a nap under the window, but the sunlight was too perfect, so I just stretched, yawned, and pretended I owned the sun.
